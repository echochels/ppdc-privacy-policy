PPDC Privacy Policy

PPDC - Professional Solar Engineers Platform

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

Introduction

PPDC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application that connects solar engineers with clients seeking solar energy services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • Complete your profile
  • Post job listings
  • Send messages to other users
  • Rate and review engineers

This information may include:

  • Full name (first, middle, last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location (state, city/town)
  • Profile photo
  • Professional certifications (e.g., COREN certification)
  • Account type (Individual or Business)

Professional Information

  • Engineering specialization
  • Project portfolio
  • Ratings and reviews
  • Job postings and applications
  • Budget information for projects
  • Service categories (PV Installation, System Design, Energy Storage, etc.)

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our app:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Log data
  • Search queries

Communications

  • Messages exchanged through our platform
  • Customer support correspondence
  • Notifications preferences

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect solar engineers with clients
  • Facilitate job postings and applications
  • Enable messaging between users
  • Process and display ratings and reviews

Communication

  • Send you notifications about jobs, messages, and platform updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send administrative information

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features
  • Monitor and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure platform security

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and safety

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Public Information

The following information is visible to other users:

  • Your name and profile photo
  • Professional information and certifications
  • Ratings and reviews
  • Location (state/city level)
  • Service categories
  • Project portfolio

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

With Other Users: When you post jobs or respond to listings, relevant profile information is shared with potential matches.

Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, customer support).

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

Please Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update

  • View and edit your profile information
  • Update your contact details and preferences

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Manage notification settings within the app

Account Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Contact us for assistance (see contact information below)

Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside Nigeria, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Nigeria or other countries where we operate.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Nigeria Data Protection Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019. We are committed to protecting the privacy rights of Nigerian citizens and residents in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Your NDPR Rights Include:

  • Right to request access to your personal data
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Consent

By using the PPDC app, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
